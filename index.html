Welcome to Remys Tailor

Remys Tailor

Family owned and operated, Remy's Tailor has been serving Lee County and the Fort Myers, Florida area for over 32 years. Remy Fenelus moved his business, Remy's Custom Design Tailor Shop, into a new plaza, the Shops at Jamaica Bay, in October 2007, leaving behind a spot he'd occupied at The Atrium Executive Center on Winkler Road for 24 years. Come and see us at our Jamaica Bay location.

At Remy's Tailor, our employees are committed to providing our customers with a quality product and a high level of service, at competitive prices. Elegance is not an accident, we work at it. Our quality fabrics, excellent fit, and the latest styles combine to create our outstanding reputation.

If you have any questions, please call us or stop by. No appointment is needed, just come on in and see Old World craftsmanship at work.

The Best Quality and Material

Remy's Tailor has been creating custom clothing and providing expert tailoring and alterations to customers throughout the Fort Myers, Florida area for over 32 years.

Each individual garment that enters Remy's Tailor follows a complete outline pertaining to the specifications and detailed tailoring instructions you wish rendered. Each item is carefully inspected upon completion as well as constantly reviewed throughout sewing to ensure they meet the proper quality standards. At various intervals the item is placed on a mannequin and inspected for balance, matching and overall appearance. Garments are measured for size accuracy and examined again before being sent out to our customers.


Our Company

With over 32 years of tailoring experience, Remy's Tailor can serve any of your tailoring needs from custom suits and clothing made from scratch, to last minute wedding alterations. No job is too small or difficult for our staff. Our experienced and professional staff is here to serve you 6 days a week!
