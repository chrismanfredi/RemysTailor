Alterations can make the difference between a garment that looks “OK” and one that looks “Great”. Our fitters and tailors have extensive knowledge about altering shirts, pants, dresses, skirts, jackets, suits, and outerwear. Our alterations are of the highest quality, but you can expect the work to be done quickly. Bring in your old pleated suits and we can update them to a modern flat front suit. Do you have a favorite garment? Bring it in with fabric and Remy's can recreate it for you. Don't have fabric? Remy's can help you with that as well.

Major Alterations

Our tailor staff is extensively trained to perform major alterations including re-cuts (for coats, and pants), sides and blades, as well as re-sizing your favorite garment.

Most garments can be significantly altered to fit when an individual looses or gains weight. Most customers are not aware of how dramatically clothes can be altered to fit perfectly after any weight change.

Standard Alterations

Remy's Tailor offers any kind of hem on pants, jeans, skirts, dresses, jackets and formal dresses.

We can replace zippers, shorten sleeves, repair straps, apply a uniform patch (military, police, school, etc.) resew buttons, and much more.
